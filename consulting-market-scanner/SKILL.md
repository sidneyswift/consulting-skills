# Consulting Market Scanner

## Steps
1. Capture niche notes, the "hungry crowd" buying signals, and underserved gaps.
2. Record market-rate references for pricing anchors.
3. Save to `positioning/market-research/{vertical}.md` (one file per vertical/theme).
4. Feed strong signals back into `positioning/icp.md` and content ideas.

Source: Ch. 3.
