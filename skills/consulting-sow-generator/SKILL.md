# Consulting SOW Generator

## Steps
1. Pull scope from the proposal/discovery and confirm the MSA is in place (`library/contracts/msa-sow-notes.md`).
2. Draft the SOW: deliverables, timeline/milestones, **payment terms**, resource requirements (key personnel, access, 48h decisions), success criteria.
3. Add relevant clauses from `library/contracts/clause-library.md`.
4. Save to `clients/{client}/03-contracts/sow-YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
5. Note the linked payment structure for invoicing.

Source: Ch. 19.
