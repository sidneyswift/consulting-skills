# Consulting Testimonial Capture

## Steps
1. Identify the specific result worth highlighting.
2. Draft the testimonial *for* the client (focused on results + value) and the request email (`library/email-templates/05-testimonial-request.md`).
3. Request logo rights and a face/photo where possible (faces + quotes beat logos).
4. Save captured testimonials to `proof/testimonials/`.
5. Note any referral opening to pursue.

Source: Ch. 14, 21.
