# Consulting Friday Review

## Steps
1. Scan `content/ideas/` (target 15–20 candidates).
2. Detect repeated questions across the week's calls and `knowledge/` — repeats are the strongest content signal.
3. Run `consulting-content-extraction` on any un-processed transcripts in `content/raw/`.
4. Propose the top 1–3 ideas to test (→ `consulting-content-idea-generator`) or draft (→ `consulting-content-drafter`).
5. Surface knowledge-base gaps (questions answered 2+ times but not yet an FAQ).
6. Report a short weekly content plan.

Source: Ch. 4 (the Friday Review).
