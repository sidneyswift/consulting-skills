# Consulting Content Idea Generator

## Steps
1. Take one core idea.
2. Generate 3–5 distinct framings (the multi-tweet test) + candidate titles.
3. Tag each by content TAM and intent; aim for the dual-reaction sweet spot (validates insiders, surprises experts).
4. Log the variants as signals in `signals/` (tag as variants of the core idea). There's no separate
   experiments folder — you "test" by drafting the strongest variants and reading what actually landed
   from the LinkedIn engagement pull (`integrations/linkedin/engagement/`). Real market voting > a speculative folder.
5. Recommend which to promote to a draft once a winner emerges.

Output: testable variants. Source: Ch. 3, 5.
