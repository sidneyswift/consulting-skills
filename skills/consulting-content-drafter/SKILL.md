# Consulting Content Drafter

## Steps
1. Take the idea/insight (often from `content/ideas/` or `knowledge/insights/`).
2. Apply the title formula: "[N] steps to [outcome] by [%] using [tool/method]"; prefer "you" over "I".
3. Structure with AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action). Lead with the problem + outcome, not your resume.
4. Place it on the specificity ladder (generic → category → specific → targeted) and match it to the business stage.
5. Save the draft to `content/drafts/` with a clear title; note the target audience (IC vs. leadership).

Output: a draft ready for review. Source: Ch. 5.
