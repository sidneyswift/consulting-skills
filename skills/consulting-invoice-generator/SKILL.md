# Consulting Invoice Generator

## Steps
1. Read the SOW's payment terms and the chosen structure (`library/pricing/payment-structures.md`).
2. Generate the invoice (amount, milestone/period, due date/net terms, payment method).
3. Confirm W-9 / billing details on file; apply prepay discount if elected.
4. Log to `business/finance/` and note it on the client dashboard.
